History

Over the past thirty years, Custom Metal Contracting Ltd. has become a leader in the architectural metals industry, developing some of the most sophisticated comprehensive building envelope systems available. Since 1980, we have proved ourselves time and time again on high-profile projects through out North America and have become known as a "one stop shop" for both the building construction and architectural design industries. We provide solutions with proven products and systems.
